Manual Tests for Webmaker Screenshot

  1. Create a new Webmaker make and submit it into the form on the Webmaker Screenshot page.

  2. After a bit of time, screenshots of your make should be displayed.

  3. Make a trivial modification to your make. Reload the Webmaker Screenshot page; the change should not be reflected in the screenshots.

  4. Click one of the "regenerate screenshots" buttons on the Webmaker Screenshot page; the related screenshots should now show the changes you made earlier.
